  Wu Wei Din: A New Chinese Favorite

   By: Timothy Do

   Published November 3, 2023


Rating: 5/5

Favorite Dish: Chicken Thigh Fried Rice

If you're looking for a place doing authentic Chinese food, look no further than Wu Wei Din in Plano! This place has quickly become one of my go-tos when I'm looking for a no-frills, well-prepared, family-style dish!

The Experience:

Every time I go to Wu Wei Din, the place is packed, and for good reason! The environment is lively and definitely a place you'd want to spend your evening eating dinner at. Something I noticed was that the wait staff was always moving around, taking orders, refilling drinks, and they would not waste their time to get the food to your table.

The Food:

The menu is an assortment of authentic Chinese food, including dumplings, soups, and rice dishes. My go to is the Chicken Thigh Fried Rice. The dish is a massive plate of fried rice, topped with an equally generous serving of a fried boneless chicken thigh. The dish is savory and perfectly seasoned, with a great crunch in the chicken.
