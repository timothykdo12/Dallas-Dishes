  Churn and Bake: An Asian-Influenced Ice Cream Shop

   By: Timothy Do

   Published November 3, 2023


Rating: 4/5

Favorite Item: Lychee Sorbet Ice Cream

Churn and Bake is an ice cream shop with Asian influence, which can be seen in their unique flavors in their ice cream and bubble waffles!

The Experience:

The environment inside felt bright but relaxed, and you can often expect to be met with a smile by the workers and manager. Samples are available if you're interested in trying any flavors. This is a great place to go for post-meal dessert for a chill vibe for conversations.

The Food:

Ice cream and bubble waffles are the main offerings at this spot. For ice cream, my favorite flavor would have to be the lychee sorbet. Fair warning to those who don't like the sweetest things, this is a very sweet flavor. For a go-to that's a bit less sweet, the ube flavor is a great pick. For the bubble waffles, the pandan flavor is my go-to. The green waffle has a very subtle taste, with an airy texture.
